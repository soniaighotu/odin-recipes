Jollof Rice

What is Jollof Rice?
Jollof rice is a beloved West African dish with many regional variations. It's made with a unique blend of spices, long-grain rice, and a tomato and red pepper base.

Here is a brief description of Jollof Rice:
Jollof Rice is a popular West African dish made with rice, tomatoes, onions, peppers, and spices. The recipe starts with sautéing onions, garlic, and ginger, followed by adding tomatoes, tomato paste, and a blend of spices, including cumin, coriander, and paprika. The mixture is then cooked until the flavors are well combined and the sauce has thickened.

Next, rice is added to the pot and stir-fried with the sauce until it's well coated. Water is then added, and the rice is cooked until tender and fluffy. Finally, the dish is seasoned with salt, pepper, and a squeeze of fresh lime juice. Jollof Rice is often served with protein sources like chicken, beef, or fish, and garnished with chopped fresh herbs like parsley or cilantro. Its flavorful and aromatic taste has made it a beloved dish across West Africa and beyond!
